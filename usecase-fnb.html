Sales Order Management

Leading Health Food and Beverage Company

Sales

"RPA technology can help the industry improve productivity, reduce costs, and enhance customer satisfaction. By automating processes such as inventory management and supply chain logistics, companies can improve operational efficiency and reduce errors."

Challenge

  • Manual Process
  • High Volume
  • Delay in order processing
  • Data entry errors
  • Resource drain

Solution

Deployed RPA using the attended robot to automate tedious process of retrieveing and verifying orders from 20 diffeernt supplier's portal (modern outlets). the robot will login to each external portal, download the orders, put them into the designated folders, extract data and verifying price and discount then enter them into Order Management module of Oracle eBusiness Suite.

Value Delivered

  • Improve efficiencies
  • Reduction of montonous work
  • Improve speed, accuracy, and quality
  • Standardized and centralized business process
  • 16x improvement in average handling time
  • Saved 16 FTE hours per day

SOLUTIONS

Increased Efficiency and Accuracy
RPA technology can automate repetitive tasks such as data entry, inventory management, and order processing. By automating these tasks, companies in the health food and beverage sector can increase their operational efficiency, reduce errors, and improve accuracy. This leads to a reduction in operational costs, faster order processing times, and increased customer satisfaction.

Enhanced Customer Service
By automating tasks such as order processing and invoicing, RPA technology frees up employees to focus on more value-added activities, such as improving customer service. This can help companies in the health food and beverage sector differentiate themselves from their competitors and build customer loyalty. Additionally, RPA can enable companies to quickly respond to customer inquiries and complaints, further enhancing the customer experience.

Improved Supply Chain Management
The health food and beverage sector is heavily reliant on a complex supply chain. RPA technology can help companies in this industry manage their supply chain more efficiently by automating tasks such as inventory management and logistics. This can lead to a reduction in inventory costs, faster delivery times, and improved inventory accuracy. Additionally, RPA can help companies in the health food and beverage sector comply with regulatory requirements, such as food safety standards, by automating processes and ensuring compliance with regulations.

Scalability
As demand for health food and beverages continues to grow, companies in this industry need to find ways to scale their operations. RPA technology can help companies in this industry scale their operations by automating routine tasks and freeing up employees to focus on innovation and growth. This can lead to faster time-to-market for new products and increased revenue.

Overall, RPA technology has the potential to transform the health food and beverage industry by increasing efficiency, improving customer service, enhancing supply chain management, and enabling scalability. Companies that adopt RPA technology are likely to gain a competitive advantage in this rapidly growing industry.
