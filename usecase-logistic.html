Billing Process Automation

Leading Fleet Management, Used Cars, and Logistics Group Companies

Billing Central

"RPA is a powerful tool for companies, enabling them to streamline processes, reduce errors, and increase productivity. As the industry becomes more competitive, those who embrace RPA will have a significant advantage."

Challenge

  • Manual Process
  • High Volume
  • Delay in billing lead time
  • Data entry errors
  • Resource Drain

Solution

Deployed RPA using the unattended robot to automate the processes within SAP of SO variable creation (VA01), invoice creation (VF04), and ensure release accounting (VF02). Also to process e-faktur pajak using excel sheet for preprocessing and daily recon; SAP (FAGLB03, FB03, ZSDE084, VF03, XD03); and e-faktur app from DJP. The robot will also create Tanda Terima Sistem (TTS) in SAP ZFF0021_N. As last step, the robot will email the documents to the designated parties.

Value Delivered

  • Enhance invoice processing and monitoring
  • Reduction of manual work
  • Improve speed, accuracy, and quality
  • Improve customer satisfaction
  • 10x improvement in average handling time
  • Saved 40 FTE hours per day

Increased Efficiency and Accuracy
RPA can automate repetitive and time-consuming tasks in the logistics industry, such as data entry, order processing, and inventory management. By automating these tasks, companies can reduce errors and improve efficiency, leading to faster and more accurate order processing. This can help companies meet customer demands more quickly, improving customer satisfaction and retention.


Cost Savings
By automating repetitive tasks, companies can reduce the need for human labor, which can lead to significant cost savings. RPA can also help companies reduce errors and avoid costly mistakes, such as incorrect shipments or lost inventory. Additionally, by improving efficiency and streamlining processes, RPA can help logistics companies reduce operational costs, such as fuel and transportation expenses.


Scalability
As logistics companies grow and expand their operations, they may struggle to keep up with increasing demands. RPA can help companies scale their operations more quickly and efficiently, without the need for additional human labor. RPA can also be easily customized to fit the specific needs of a logistics company, making it a flexible and adaptable solution.

Improved Data Management
In the logistics industry, data management is crucial for tracking shipments, managing inventory, and analyzing customer trends. RPA can help companies manage their data more effectively by automating data entry, data extraction, and data processing. This can help companies identify trends and patterns more quickly, leading to better decision-making and improved customer service.


Enhanced Customer Service
By improving efficiency and accuracy, RPA can help logistics companies provide better customer service. RPA can automate order processing, shipment tracking, and inventory management, allowing companies to provide real-time updates to customers. This can lead to faster and more accurate deliveries, which can improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.


In summary, RPA can bring numerous benefits to the logistics industry, including increased efficiency, cost savings, scalability, improved data management, and enhanced customer service. By automating repetitive tasks, logistics companies can improve their operational efficiency, reduce errors, and better serve their customers.
